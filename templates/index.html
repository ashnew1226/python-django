ABOUT US

Founded in 2021, KP Infotech Vidhyapeeth is a skill development initiative of KP Group, with its head office in Pune. It aims to train the people in the rural areas of Maharashtra into a skilled workforce for employment, entrepreneurship, and community enterprise, through vocational education.

Our Works

The estimated population of Maharashtra in 2021 is 124.7 Million (12.47 Crores), making it the second-most populous state in India after Uttar Pradesh. 9% of India's population lives in Maharashtra. The economic benefit of having such a large working-age population is tremendous not just for Maharashtra but for India as well. However, a significant percentage of this population is unskilled or under-skilled, especially in the rural and underprivileged areas of Maharashtra. This is why Skill building is a key focus of KP Infotech Vidhyapeeth. The transformation of underskilled or unskilled individuals into employees or businessmen involves interpersonal skills, updated knowledge, experience, and resources. KP Infotech Vidhyapeeth will provide both quality educational and financial support required to uplift the rural population of Maharashtra into skilled individuals. It aims to promote skill development by facilitating the creation of large, quality vocational institutions.

Our Mission

KP Infotech Vidhyapeeth’s mission is to make at least 1 lac individuals, Self-dependent and Self-reliant, in the underdeveloped, financially challenged, and rural areas of Maharashtra. We aim to reach out to these communities, develop the skills of people and create at least 1000 entrepreneurs in the next 1000 days. By consistent and healthy growth, we aim to create 1800 centers for training and skill development in Maharashtra and appoint at least 1100 highly experienced & highly qualified trainers in these centers. Every District will have a center facilitating a wide range of both on-field and online courses that would help transform these aspiring individuals into skilled employees or venturesome entrepreneurs across various industries. Different courses aimed at training in different skills will be customized based on the needs of the particular region. This will ensure maximum benefit to the motivated, skilled individuals looking for employment or setting up their own business. It is KP Infotech Vidhyapeeth’s motto to provide each individual gain their education, acquire a job, or set up their business within 15 kilometers from their place of residence.

Our Vision

We care deeply about the community. Growing together as a community is what we aspire for. Hence, our dream is to facilitate employment, entrepreneurship, or community enterprise opportunities and enrich at least one lac faces with a smile of contentment. We want to be an organization that is recognized for its happy customer and people practices and is acknowledged for fostering a long-term relationship with stakeholders. That is how we define success. Ambitious as it sounds but we are fully groomed with both professional and personal ethics to achieve such milestones.

KP Infotech Vidyapeeth
